End of life admin made simple.

Launching soon.

When someone dies they leave behind a number of accounts which need to be closed — utilities, banking, boardband, insurance, the list goes on. Settld was born out of our team's personal experience of loss, and our frustration at spending hours contacting each company and waiting weeks for confirmation of acount closure. Launching in July 2020, Settld has one simple goal: cut the bereavment red tape and remove the stress for others, free of charge.

Support our #BereavementStandard campaign to help us take the complexity out of end of life admin.
